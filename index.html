ABOUT

My Background

My path into tech began with my studies in physics, where I developed an appreciation for complex systems and honed my analytical mindset through coding in Python. Physics taught me to stay curious and tackle challenges with resilience — skills that prove powerful in web development on a daily basis.

Beyond the technical side, I bring experience in tutoring and social media management. As a tutor, I learned the value of clear communication and empathy, creating study plans tailored to each student's needs to not only achieve lasting improvements in their grades, but also boost their confidence.
In my role as a social media manager for the student organization IAESTE, I designed a lasting series of engaging posts and interactive stories, strengthen their online presence and help them stand out among numerous students organizations.

During an international internship in Tanzania, I had the chance to contribute to STEM education for young girls and supported teacher development across the country. I co-hosted an interactive Astronomy Day for over 200 students, sparking curiosity about science, and shared my knowledge of astronomy with teachers to deepen their subject expertise.

Why I became a Developer

Transitioning into web development has been an exciting shift, allowing me to join a field that's constantly evolving and filled with opportunities for growth. I'm committed to contribute to projects that promote sustainability, environmental awareness, and social justice. Through web development, I aim to create applications that engage and inform for a positive change in the world.

While I work well independently, I truly thrive in collaborative environments where we can support and encourage each other. I value empathy and a positive working culture, as I believe they're key to creating meaningful work.

By staying curious and open to new ideas, I aim to create digital experiences that inspire a better tomorrow.
